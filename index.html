World Health Organization Declares Obesity a Global Epidemic
The Globe and Mail • 2000 • Foreign Desk, Geneva

GENEVA — The WHO has published a major report officially declaring obesity a global epidemic, warning that rates are climbing fast in both rich and poor countries alike, three years after the diet drug fen-phen was pulled from shelves over heart valve damage.

"We have a rapidly worsening health crisis and almost no medical tool that people trust to fix it," said one WHO nutrition advisor involved in drafting the report.
